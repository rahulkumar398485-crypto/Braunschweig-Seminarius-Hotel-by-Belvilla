Comfort & Convenience

Braunschweig Seminarius Hotel by Belvilla offers welcoming accommodation for business travelers and vacation guests. Choose from Standard Rooms, Comfort Rooms and spacious Studio Rooms designed with modern interiors, workspaces and relaxing amenities.
