Wellness

A Relaxing Escape for Body & Mind

In our wellness area, you can relax and leave your daily worries behind. Treat yourself to some downtime in our sauna and relaxation area. Right along the canal near the hotel, you'll find beautiful running routes for your outdoor workouts. Guests can also use the gym next to the hotel for a small fee of €5.00.
