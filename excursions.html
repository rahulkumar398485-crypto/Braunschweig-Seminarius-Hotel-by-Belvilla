Discover Braunschweig

Culture, Nature & New Experiences

Braunschweig is the perfect destination for a varied weekend getaway. The “City of Lions” charms visitors with its historic old town, cathedral, Dankwarderode Castle and cozy cafés. Nature lovers will enjoy walks in Bürgerpark or the idyllic village of Riddagshausen. The surrounding area also offers exciting destinations including the Autostadt in Wolfsburg, museums, cycling and hiking trails and many cultural highlights.
