City Adventures

Experience the Best of Braunschweig

Braunschweig combines historic landmarks, charming cafés, green parks and cultural highlights. Guests can explore cathedrals, castles, museums and nearby nature areas while enjoying a perfect mix of city life and relaxation.

Top Attractions

Discover the Region

Braunschweig Cathedral

One of the city's most iconic landmarks with impressive medieval architecture and historical significance.

Dankwarderode Castle

A reconstructed medieval castle showcasing the rich history of Braunschweig and the region.

Autostadt Wolfsburg

A modern automotive and cultural destination with exhibitions, experiences and family-friendly attractions.

Riddagshausen Nature Area

A peaceful nature reserve perfect for relaxing walks, cycling and discovering local wildlife.

Bürgerpark

A beautiful green park ideal for outdoor activities, jogging and relaxing moments in nature.
