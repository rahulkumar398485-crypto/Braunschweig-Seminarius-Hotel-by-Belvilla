Getting Here

Travel to Braunschweig Seminarius Hotel by Belvilla

Braunschweig Central Station offers convenient regional and long-distance train connections to the city.

The hotel is easily accessible via the A2 highway and offers convenient access for business and leisure travelers.

Braunschweig-Wolfsburg Airport is located just a few minutes from the hotel.
