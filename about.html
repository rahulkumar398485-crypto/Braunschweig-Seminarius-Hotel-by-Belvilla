Our Story

Braunschweig Seminarius Hotel by Belvilla

Comfortable & Convenient Location

Braunschweig Seminarius Hotel by Belvilla is located in a quiet and easily accessible area near the A2 highway and just minutes from Braunschweig-Wolfsburg Airport. The hotel offers a welcoming atmosphere for both business and leisure travelers.

A Stay Designed for Every Guest

Our hotel features Standard Rooms, Comfort Rooms and spacious Studio Rooms with modern amenities, comfortable interiors and thoughtful details. Whether for conferences, family celebrations or relaxing weekends, guests enjoy comfort and convenience throughout their stay.
